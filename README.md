# 📋 TrackIt

**Stay organized, one task at a time.**

TrackIt is a modern and simple TODO application with a clean dashboard to help you manage your daily tasks.

## 🚀 Live Demo
[View TrackIt Live](https://lezinsaajid.github.io/TrackIt/)

---

## ✨ Features
- **Dashboard Stats**: See your total, completed, pending, and overdue tasks at a glance.
- **Task Management**: Add tasks with descriptions, categories, and priority levels.
- **Light & Dark Mode**: Switch themes for a better viewing experience.
- **Offline Storage**: Your tasks are saved locally on your browser.
- **PWA Support**: Can be installed as an app on your phone or desktop.
- **Search & Filter**: Find your tasks quickly.
